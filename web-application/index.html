Project Overview

The aim of this project is to visualize career transitions in the IT industry over the past decades. By tracking the movement of employees between companies and highlighting different occupations, we want to provide insight into industry trends, labor mobility, and career patterns.

Key Objectives:

  • Track frequency of company changes
  • Analyze industry evolution over time
  • Identify career centers and stepping stones
  • Monitor skill popularity trends
  • Analyze occupational category changes
